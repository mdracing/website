Audi B5 RS4 | Forge Action Day @ Castle Combe | 08/09/12

This summers Castle Combe Action Day was sponsored by the great guys @ Forge Motorsport so it would be rude not to go and check the day out. Weather was stunning and very hot indeed, which made for a very very busy day... to say it was busy was an understatement, it took 40 minutes just to get in the gate!!

Track was fun as always though, busy but entertaining which you get a feeling for in the movies at the bottom of the page.

Got a few snaps of the car in the paddock and most of the other pictures are thanks to Andy Smith @ Lensflair.
