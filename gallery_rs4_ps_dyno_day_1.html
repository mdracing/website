Audi B5 RS4 | Dyno Day @ PowerStation | 09/06/12

Seeing as it had been a while since the last organised Dyno Day I set about organising one at PowerStation for the guys on the AudiSRS.com and S2 forums. Initial response was great but as can happen people dropped out along the line with car issues or other commitments so the day ended up fairly small but was still great fun.

There were 9 runners in the end and the day didn't start too well when the first two cars (both B5 RS4's) produced considerably lower than expected figures with estimates at around 460bhp and readings of 400bhp. Seems it may have just highlighted either "generous" previous dyno results or a few things that need to be checked out on the cars concerned.

Thankfully a couple of the cars produced more than expected confirming the rollers were doing there job on the day. For me, it was time to see just what the mapping by Rick @ Unicorn had produced. Results were fantastic, better than expected so I'm well chuffed. There will be a separate writeup on my results along with videos shortly.

The cars which ran are below in their running order with their mods and expected outputs followed by their actual results and graphs.

Audi B5 RS4 | Owned by Dane

Mods: Milltek DP's with CAT's & Full System, MRC Map
Expected: 466 bhp
Results: 400 bhp / 417 lb.ft



Audi B5 RS4 | Owned by Rich

Mods: Milltek DP's with CAT's & Full System, MRC Map
Expected: 466 bhp
Results: 396 bhp / 418 lb.ft



Audi B5 S4 | Owned by Tony

Mods: RS4 Intercoolers, RS4 DP with Gutted Pre-Cats, QST (Scorpion) System, Unknown Map
Expected: 325 bhp
Results: 337 bhp / 363 lb.ft



Audi B5 RS4 | Owned by Jon (me!)

Mods: Bosch 044 Fuel Pump, Bosch EV14 750cc Injectors, Bosch F5DP0R Plugs, K&N Induction Filter, Silicone MAF Hose, DevilsOwn Water/Meth Injection, Wagner Sidemount Intercoolers, Milltek 3" Downpipes (no CATs), Milltek Exhaust System, Unicorn Multiple Maps, Flat Shift, Launch Control
Expected: 500+ bhp
Results: Three different maps were run back to back in the order below with the car getting hotter each time...
537 bhp / 515 lb.ft (Meth Map)
495 bhp / 449 lb.ft (No Meth Map)
412 bhp / 334 lb.ft (Low Boost Map)



Audi C6 S6 | Owned by Barry

Mods: Custom CAT-Back Exhaust, Filters, Map
Expected: 435 bhp
Results: Two runs were made to see if the Sport/Auto modes made a difference
363 bhp / 337 lb.ft (Sport)
367 bhp / 332 lb.ft (Auto)




Audi B7 2.0 FSiT | Owned by Andy

Mods: Revo Map
Expected: 260 bhp
Results: 243 bhp / 291 lb.ft



Audi B6 S4 | Owned by Richard

Mods: Standard
Expected: 344 bhp
Results: 309 bhp / 270 lb.ft



Audi B5 RS4 | Owned by Phil

Mods: Wagner 3" DP's with CATs, SuperSprint CAT-Back System, ITG Panel Filter, Wagner Sidemount Intercoolers, MRC Map
Expected: 470 bhp
Results: 443 bhp / 425 lb.ft



Audi B4 RS2 | Owned by Ross

Mods: 3" De-Catted System, K26/27 Turbo, Bosch EV14 Injectors, Map
Expected: 400 bhp
Results: 351 bhp / 399 lb.ft
