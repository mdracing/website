Audi B5 RS4 | Milltek Exhaust System & 3" Downpipes | 14/09/11

The long term plan has always been to fit a set of 3" Downpipes without CATS along with an associated system and the opportunity arose to obtain a full Milltek System which was too good to pass up. Brand new and never fitted I can't wait to get them on the car!


Fitting | 20/09/11

Having sorted a full Milltek Exhaust System & 3" Downpipes it was over to the chaps at e-parts to get the system fitted. I don't envy them, it's a tough job doing the downpipes at the best of times! I stuck around of a bit but left them too it, will be collecting tomorrow and can't wait.
