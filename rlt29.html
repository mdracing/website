GENERAL DESCRIPTION
The RLT29 features a Swiss ETA 7 jewelled quartz movement housed in a 42mm, all steel, early 1990's prototype TAG Heuer case with ratchet bezel, screw-on back, signed screw-down crown and sapphire crystal. Water resistant to 200 metres, the watch was available with 16 different dial colour schemes, 8 different hour/minute hand sets and 6 different seconds hand types, giving 768 possible combinations although probably less than 100 watches were produced. RLT29 was supplied as standard on a rubber strap.
