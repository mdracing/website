Audi B5 RS4 | Plastidip Coating Sportec Wheels | 04/11/12

When I bought the Sportec wheels they were quite cheap as the refurb job on them was shocking! I never really planned to keep them in that condition and indeed after a weekâ€™s use the fronts were badly marked from the DS2500 brake dust, as you can see from the pics below. Yes, those have been cleaned very well and that's staining that's been left!

A while back I started to hear about this stuff called Plastidip which has been around for some time now but only in the past few years have people started to use it on vehicle parts. Whilst the colour range of the spray cans are a bit limiting I thought I'd just give it a go with the straight black and see how it went. The idea of this stuff is that it's pretty simple to remove, you just peel it off... well time will tell about that but there are plenty of YouTube videos about Plastidip so go take a look.

As said, I cleaned the wheels thoroughly and you can see the fronts are quite marked whilst the rears are pretty much OK (though still have the terrible refurb finish). I then used the "index card" technique for covering the tyres, though plenty of people don't bother at all as you can peel the stuff back off the tyres but I figure this way is easier. Then it's just a case of give the wheels several coats of Plastidip starting off quite thin and then building it up.

End result looks superb IMO, very pleased with them and they will be going on the car for the next trackday...
