Audi B5 RS4 | Santa Pod RWYB | 28/10/12

There were a few folk chatting on AudiSRS.com about going along to the final RWYB (Run What You Brung) event at Santa Pod and I decided to pop along and see what the old girl could do!

Being October it's a bit risky with the weather and Sunday didn't disappoint, it was cooooold (good for engines) and a little damp (not good for traction). It had rained heavily overnight so the first hour was spent drying the track off but allowed us to get signed in and in the queue, number 2 as it happens which was nice but also meant I'd be first down the strip!

Managed to get 8 runs in and the times were all over the place! Hardly seemed able to link up a decent launch and then decent gear changes, sometimes it felt like I had a box of neutrals!! Think it's time to either fit the JHM Short Shift or replace all the mechanism with new OEM stuff. The one run that hooked up best resulted in a 12.35 @ 114.79 mph which given the conditions I was pretty happy with. I felt there was a better time in there somewhere but the queue was massive by that time and not long after it started to rain so that was pretty much the end of the day for me.

As it happened the final 2012 round of the Monster Energy Gymkhana Grid so there were people like Ken Block and Liam Doran doing their stuff, I only strolled over for a little look once and it just happened that Block and Doran were doing their stuff then which was a nice bonus to the day!

Loved the day on the whole, gotta improve the time though and for that it does mean having to wait until at least late January as it's now closed until the new year... boo!
